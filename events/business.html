The Event

Our regular monthly coffee and chat. As normal, there's no set agenda: just turn up and discuss ruby in the business community. We could do a round table where anyone can put forward a topic of their interest and they get feedback from the group on specific issues.

The Goal

This meetup is for founders, CEO and directors who run their operations with Ruby.

These meet up focuses on providing complementary advice to ruby developers beyond ruby such as, career advice, state of the ruby market , how to become a contractor, how to start a tech startup, how to build a team, new tech trends, etc.
